Styleguide

So that all our project sheets look the same and read as if they are written with one voice here's some guidelines.

General

As a base we use the Economist style guide. Variations from that are detailed below.

Measurements

All measurements are to be in SI units or SI derivatives.

Areas are in m² not M², M2, m2, sqm, sq.m etc.

loads more stylistic ranting to come here!

Spacing

All multiple spaces will be colapsed to single spaces by the browser, but for consistency, single space after full stops.

Don't add in empty lines between paragraphs. Extra spacing around paragraphs will be provided by the styling at presentation time and will be adjustable. If you do put in an extra line then that will throw off the spacing of the paragraphs in a way that we won't be able to fix.
